PRO SE GUIDEBOOK

Knowledge is your weapon. Structure is your shield.

⚔ First Rule

Do not rely on memory. Courts care about records, dates, documents, contradictions, and proof. If it matters—write it down.

📖 What Pro Se Means

Representing yourself is not guessing or ranting. It is building a disciplined record that can withstand scrutiny.

🛡 Core Weapons

  • Timeline of events
  • Evidence index
  • Written communications
  • Contradictions in statements
  • Deadlines and filings

🎯 Objective

You are not telling a story. You are building a record that forces truth to reveal itself.
