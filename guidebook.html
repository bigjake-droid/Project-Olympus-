Pro Se Guidebook

Knowledge is your weapon. Structure is your shield.

First Rule

Do not rely on memory alone. Courts care about records, dates, documents, contradictions, and proof. If it matters, write it down.

What Pro Se Means

Pro se means representing yourself without an attorney. That does not mean guessing, ranting, or winging it. It means building a disciplined record.

Your Core Weapons

  • Timeline of events
  • Evidence index
  • Written communications
  • Contradictions in reports or statements
  • Deadlines and filing dates

Do Not Do This

  • Do not miss deadlines.
  • Do not rely on verbal promises.
  • Do not submit disorganized evidence.
  • Do not mix emotion with factual claims.

Vindex Principle

A clean record is harder to bury. A clean timeline is harder to twist. A clean evidence packet forces the other side to answer facts.
