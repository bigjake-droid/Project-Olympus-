PRO SE GUIDEBOOK

A system, not a guess.

Records win cases. Memory loses them.

⚔ First Rule

Do not rely on memory. Courts care about records, dates, documents, contradictions, and proof. If it matters—write it down.

📖 What Pro Se Means

Pro se means representing yourself. Not guessing. Not ranting. It means building a disciplined record.

🛡 Core Weapons

  • Timeline of events
  • Evidence index
  • Written communication
  • Contradictions in statements
  • Deadlines and filings

🎯 Objective

You are not telling a story. You are building a record that forces truth.
